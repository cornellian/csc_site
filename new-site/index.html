About Us

Project Structure

VCG engages with its clients on 12 to 14 week long projects. Each team is assigned a project leader, 4-6 consultants, and 1-2 senior advisors. We regularly meet and deliberate with all of our clients to keep our work consistently relevant and as effectual as possible. In addition to our regular engagement, VCG consulting teams compile and present at least two formal deliverables. Additional midpoint deliverables can also be scheduled at our client's request.
