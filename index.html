About

We are a student led group with motivated, entrepreneurial, and talented individuals who provide pro-bono consulting work for organizations in the area of strategic consulting, finance, marketing, and many other fields.

Diversity

Our teams consist of the most creative, analytical, and driven students at Cornell. We accept all majors, and have analysts in each of the seven schools. This allows us to provide the widest range of perspective in solving client issues.

Community

We want to make a difference in the Cornell and Ithaca community through our consulting work and initiatives by being the only consulting group at Cornell that also focuses on helping start up companies in the Ithaca community.
